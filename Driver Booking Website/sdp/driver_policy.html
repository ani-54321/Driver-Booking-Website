      Driver's Policies

  1. Drivers must have a valid driver's license for the type of vehicle to be operated, and keep the license(s) with them at all times while driving. All CDL drivers must comply with all applicable D.O.T. regulations, including successful completion of medical, drug, and alcohol evaluations.

  2. Company vehicles are to be driven by authorized employees ONLY, except in emergencies, or in case of repair testing by a mechanic. Other employees and family members are not authorized to drive the Company vehicle.

  3. Company vehicles are to be driven for Company Business ONLY. Personal use of company vehicles is prohibited. No unauthorized persons are allowed to ride in company vehicles.

  4. Any kind of harassment to Passengers will not be entertained and Drivers may have to pay for this. The same is for Passengers.

  5. Drivers should not look into internal affairs of Passenger or Passenger's Family without his/her Permission.

  6. Driver should have valid proof for innosence in above cases and he should submit that in case of False Abuses.

  7. In any case when Driver is inocent, company will Save him from False Charges and will continue his/her job without any penulties.

  8. Driver should not pay any kind of toll and if you are forced then call the Company Care.

  9. If anything bad happens call emergency number given on contact us.
